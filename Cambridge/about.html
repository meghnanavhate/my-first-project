Vision & Values

The real motivation for any student is not just grades, it is the spirit of learning and fostering a mindset for deep educational curiosity. This is our belief at Cambridge International School. We want to raise young global citizens who believe in having a strong character inside and outside the classroom.

Our vision is purposeful and keeps growth at the epicentre of all our efforts.

Our three cornerstones are humility, resilience, and kindness. We believe in maintaining dignity in whatever we do and that tomorrow’s India is built in our classrooms requiring our careful collaboration and dedication.

Leadership

Cambridge International School along with its 9 sister schools is part of the Saint Lawrence group of schools set up in 1988 by Chairman Collins Albuquerque and Vice-Chairperson Mrs. Flavia Albuquerque.

Through years of dedication and hard work, this duo has imparted world-class education to over 50,000 students across Maharashtra and Karnataka. With blessings from above and the unyielding support of Chh. Sambhajinagar, they kickstarted their foray into the field of education.

This new venture at Pune helps continue our mission to provide the best for India's future! We are bold in our ambition and uphold the highest standards for our children’s most formative years of learning.

Principal’s Message

Growing up around educationists makes my thirst for learning unquenchable. I aspire to be a student all my life—learning to grow, lead, and help Cambridgites reach new milestones.

The need of the hour is to help our brilliant minds reach their true potential—and that is where I, as a Principal, and CIS as a school come in. Let us together build an institution that is synonymous with excellence!

Principal: Vishaka Albuquerque
