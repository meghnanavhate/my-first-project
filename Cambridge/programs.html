Kindergarten - Where Everything Begins

At CIS, we understand the value of the trust that young parents place on us. Our focus in these years is to help our little new Cambridgites find their feet and forge friendships within a collaborative and supportive environment. Kindness and inclusivity are the lifeblood of our thriving Kindergarten.

Our youngest Cambridgites are introduced to an interactive and broad curriculum, giving them enough time to familiarize themselves with new concepts and subjects. Our trained and qualified teachers help our students find their rhythm and grow.

Primary - Where Everything Grows

New classmates, new subjects, new teachers, and a whole new environment. We recognize that primary students require support and encouragement as they start to build their learning styles and establish a relationship with school.

As young Cambridgites, we want our students to navigate school life with a certain confidence. We combine outdoor and indoor learning to set our students up for success in high school.
